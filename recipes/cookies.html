Ginger Cookies

Big, soft, gingerbread cookies made with molasses, ginger, and other spices are a holiday favorite. This gingerbread cookie recipe for ginger cookies rolled in sugar is my oldest son's favorite. Store cooled cookies in an airtight container and they will stay soft for days.

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Spices
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Margarine
  • Egg
  • Molasses
  1. Sift the dry ingredients together
  2. Cream the margarine and suger together. Beat in the egg, then add the water and molasses
  3. Stir the dry ingredients into wet ingredients until well-combined
  4. Shape the dough into balls, roll the balls into sugar, and flatten them slightly.
  5. Bake in a preheated over until almost set and cool on a wire rack.
